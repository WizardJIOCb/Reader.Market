READER.MARKET — LANDING PAGE SPECIFICATION
===================================

Goal
----
Explain the product in 10–15 seconds:
Reader.market is an AI-powered reading service that helps users understand and remember their own books.

Primary conversion: Sign up / Get started.


1. General Requirements
-----------------------
- Landing must clearly state:
  - Users upload THEIR OWN books
  - This is NOT a bookstore or marketplace
  - AI is used for summaries, insights, and recommendations
- Tone: clear, honest, early-stage but confident
- Audience: non-fiction readers, professionals, learners, early adopters

Technical:
- Static HTML or SSR for the landing (important for SEO)
- JS hydration allowed after initial render
- Provide meta tags (title, description, OpenGraph)
- Page must be readable without JS


2. Page Structure (Blocks)
--------------------------

2.1 Hero Block
--------------
Headline:
"Read smarter with AI-powered insights from your books"

Subheadline:
"Upload your own books and turn them into structured knowledge with summaries, key ideas, and personalized recommendations."

CTA:
- Primary: Get started — free
- Secondary: See how it works


2.2 How It Works
----------------
3-step explanation:

1. Upload your book
   Add your own books to your personal library (FB2 supported).

2. Read with AI assistance
   Get summaries, key ideas, and explanations as you read.

3. Remember more & discover next reads
   Save insights and get recommendations based on what you actually read.


2.3 What AI Helps You Do
-----------------------
Bullet list:
- Get concise chapter summaries
- Extract key ideas and insights
- Understand complex books faster
- Receive personalized reading recommendations


2.4 Why Reader.market
---------------------
Key points:
- Built for understanding, not just reading pages
- Your personal library — not a bookstore
- Privacy-first, fast AI processing
- Ideal for non-fiction and deep reading


2.5 Who It Is For
-----------------
Audience list:
- Non-fiction readers
- Students and lifelong learners
- Professionals who read to grow
- People who want to remember what they read


2.6 Formats & Roadmap
---------------------
Supported:
- FB2

Coming soon:
- EPUB
- PDF


2.7 Early-stage Transparency
----------------------------
Text:
"Reader.market is an early-stage product.
We actively develop it and shape new features based on user feedback."


2.8 Final CTA
-------------
Text:
"Start reading smarter. Turn books into knowledge with AI."

CTA:
Get started — free


3. Optional: Early Adopter Version
----------------------------------
May exist as /early or ?early=1

Hero (alternative):
"An AI reading tool for people who care about understanding books"

Subheadline:
"Early-stage AI-powered reader. Upload your books, explore summaries and insights, and help shape the future of smart reading."

CTA:
Join early access


4. SEO & Metadata
-----------------
Title:
Reader.market — AI-powered book reading and insights

Meta description:
AI-powered reading service to analyze your books with summaries, key ideas, and personalized recommendations.

Add:
- OpenGraph title & description
- Simple FAQ schema (2–3 questions)


5. Non-goals
------------
- No pricing yet (unless already implemented)
- No promises of unsupported features
- No marketplace language


6. Success Criteria
-------------------
A first-time visitor should be able to answer:
- What is this?
- Is it for my own books?
- What does AI do here?
- Why should I sign up?
